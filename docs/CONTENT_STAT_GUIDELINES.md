## ğŸ§  How to Think About Stat Boxes

### Ask Yourself:

**Bad question:** "What's interesting about our keyword research?"  
**Good question:** "What does the reader need to know to understand their situation?"

**Bad question:** "How can we show this keyword is trending?"  
**Good question:** "How many people are affected by this issue?"

**Bad question:** "What search volume should we highlight?"  
**Good question:** "What validates this is a real, widespread problem?"

### The User Perspective Test:

Before adding a stat, imagine you're the reader and ask:
- "Does this help me understand if I'm affected?"
- "Does this help me understand the scale of the issue?"
- "Does this help me decide whether to take action?"
- "Does this make me trust the information more?"

If the answer is NO to all four â†’ Don't use it!

**Wrong thinking:** "High search volume proves this is important"
