## üí° News Content Best Practices

### **1. Date Everything Clearly**

**Good:** "On 12 December 2025, the FCA closed consultation on CP25/27..."  
**Bad:** "The FCA recently announced..."

**Why:** Specificity = authority, clarity, SEO value

### **2. Source Everything**

**Link to:** FCA official releases, policy statements, press releases  
**Quote:** Official FCA language  
**Reference:** CP25/27 document numbers

**Why:** Credibility, compliance, backlink opportunities

### **3. Explain Practical Impact**

**Not just:** "FCA announced X"  
**But:** "FCA announced X, which means you can now Y"

**Why:** Serve search intent (what does this mean for ME?)

### **4. Update Regularly**

**News posts need:**
- "Updated: [date]" at top
- New information added as announced
- Keep current through May 2026

**Why:** Fresh content ranks better, shows you're maintaining it

### **5. Link to Your Claim Guides**

**Every FCA post should:**
- Link to PCP claims guide
- Link to lender guides
- Link to how to claim
- Funnel readers from news ‚Üí action

**Why:** Convert news readers into claimants
